Colleen's Favorite Dance Movies

My top 3 Dance Movies

Singing in the Rain

A classic Fred Astaire and Gene Kelley tap dancing musical masterpiece.

Center Stage

Contemporay dance struggle for a ballerina to break out of the confines of ballet and seek her place in a ballet company.

Save the last dance

Troubling home life leaves Julia Stiles with out a clear path to continue her love of ballet dance. She make a friend that introduces her to hip hop and finds her new love of dance.
